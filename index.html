Who are we...?

The University of Ibadan law students' society, fondly called LSSUI or UNIBADANLSS is the representative body for all University of Ibadan law students. Its objectives include the representation of all University of Ibadan law students in academic, social and politics issues. It serves as an avenue to express their voices and explore extra-currilular activities beneficial to becoming better persons.
